About the Nova Scotia PNP

Nova Scotia Nominee Program (NSNP)

The Nova Scotia Nominee Program (NSNP) is an economic immigration program that helps the province nominate skilled workers, entrepreneurs and graduates who want to live and work in Nova Scotia permanently.

Minimum Requirements

To be eligible for most NSNP streams, you must:

  • Score at least 67 points out of 100 on the NSNP points grid
  • Have the education, work experience and language ability to economically establish in Nova Scotia
  • Have a full-time, permanent job offer from a Nova Scotia employer (for some streams)
  • Have sufficient settlement funds
  • Meet the specific requirements of the stream you're applying under

Popular NSNP Streams

  • Nova Scotia Experience: Express Entry - For individuals with work experience in Nova Scotia
  • Nova Scotia Labour Market Priorities - Targets specific occupations in demand
  • Physician Stream - For physicians offered employment in Nova Scotia
  • Entrepreneur Stream - For experienced business owners/entrepreneurs
  • International Graduate Entrepreneur Stream - For international graduates who want to start a business

Next Steps After Calculating Your Points

If you meet the minimum 67-point threshold:

  1. Review the specific requirements for the NSNP stream that matches your profile
  2. Prepare your documents (language tests, educational credentials, work references, etc.)
  3. Create an Expression of Interest profile if required
  4. Submit your application when the stream opens
