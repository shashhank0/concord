Common Contact Questions

We offer a free 15-minute initial consultation to assess your situation and determine how we can help. For more in-depth consultations (30-60 minutes), we charge a fee that can be applied toward future services if you choose to work with us.

For the most productive consultation, please have available: your passport details, education credentials, work experience details, language test results (if any), and any previous immigration applications or refusals.

No reputable immigration consultant can guarantee approval as all decisions are made by IRCC officers. However, we can ensure your application is complete, accurate, and presents your case in the strongest possible way.

Our fees vary depending on the complexity of your case and the services required. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. After our initial assessment, we'll provide a detailed fee estimate before you commit to anything.
