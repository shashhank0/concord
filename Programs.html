Canadian Immigration Programs

Explore all pathways to permanent residency in Canada

Express Entry System

Canada's flagship immigration management system for skilled workers. It includes three main programs:

Federal Skilled Worker Program

  • For skilled workers with foreign work experience
  • Minimum 1 year continuous full-time work experience
  • Language proficiency required (CLB 7)
  • Education assessment required
Learn More

Federal Skilled Trades Program

  • For qualified tradespersons
  • Minimum 2 years full-time work experience
  • Valid job offer or certificate of qualification
  • Language proficiency required (CLB 5)
Learn More

Canadian Experience Class

  • For those with Canadian work experience
  • Minimum 1 year skilled work in Canada
  • Language proficiency required (CLB 7 for TEER 0/1, CLB 5 for TEER 2/3)
  • No education requirement
Learn More

Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP)

Each Canadian province and territory (except Quebec) has its own immigration programs targeting specific local labor market needs.

Select a province or territory to view its specific immigration programs.

Family Sponsorship Programs

Canadian citizens and permanent residents can sponsor certain family members to come to Canada.

Spouse/Partner Sponsorship

  • Sponsor your spouse, common-law or conjugal partner
  • Minimum 3 year undertaking period
  • Sponsor must meet income requirements
Learn More

Parent/Grandparent Sponsorship

  • Sponsor your parents and grandparents
  • Limited application spots each year
  • Strict income requirements
  • 20 year undertaking period
Learn More

Other Family Members

  • Dependent children (including adopted)
  • Orphaned relatives under 18
  • Other relatives under specific circumstances
Learn More
