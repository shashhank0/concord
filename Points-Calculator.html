What does this score mean?

Your score will be assessed against the CRS cut-off in Express Entry draws. Recent draws have had cut-offs ranging from 450-480 points.

Recommendations to Improve Your Score

  • Improve your language test scores, particularly in your first official language
  • Gain additional Canadian work experience
  • Pursue higher education or Canadian education
  • Obtain a provincial nomination (adds 600 points)
  • Secure a valid job offer from a Canadian employer

About the CRS Score

The Comprehensive Ranking System is used to assess and score your profile for Express Entry. Scores range from 0 to 1200.

Recent Draw Cutoffs

Latest CRS cutoffs: 472 (All-program), 358 (PNP-only), 486 (CEC-only). Updated weekly.

Improving Your Score

Ways to increase your score: improve language tests, gain Canadian experience, get a provincial nomination.
