Frequently Asked Questions

The minimum CRS score changes with each draw. In 2025, the cutoff scores have ranged between 450-480 for all-program draws. However, some Provincial Nominee Program (PNP) draws have had lower cutoffs. Check our latest draw results for current information.

IRCC aims to process most Express Entry applications within 6 months from the date of receiving a complete application. However, this can vary depending on individual circumstances and application volume.

Yes, a job offer is not required for the Federal Skilled Worker Program or Canadian Experience Class. However, having a valid job offer can give you additional CRS points (50 or 200 depending on the NOC code).

Common ways to improve your score include: improving language test results, gaining more work experience, obtaining Canadian education credentials, getting a provincial nomination (600 points), securing a valid job offer, or having a sibling in Canada.
